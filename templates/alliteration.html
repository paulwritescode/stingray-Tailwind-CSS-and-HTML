The great light...

Sitting blindly in a dark corner,
You are all I could think of,
Thoughts of you brought me peace,
Thoughts of you calmed my heart.
You are the light I saw in the darkness,
and now you are the light i behold everyday.
You are the thread I hang on by,
Your smile, its so beautiful it draws me in.
Everyday I dream of making memories with you,
Memories as happy as they can be,
its a prayer that I have,
and I know it will be
